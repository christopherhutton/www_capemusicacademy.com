Why Trust Me To Teach You?

Well This is Me:

Since the age of ten years old I have played guitar, piano and drums, thus began my musical journey.  Around five years ago, 2014, I started to produce music with FL Studio, this is a piece of software that allows you to electronically produce music.

 I am a fully self taught artist and I had to learn the software through years of trial and error.  I spent about 3-4 years just learning what everything did and as time went by my passion for Electronic Music grew more and more.  I figured it all out in about 4 years, and now I'm finally able to produce music that I love and not worry about learning the boring side of things, I simply open my laptop and just make music...it's an extention of my brain.

At the moment I'm sending my orignal music to Record Labels all over the world with the aim of becoming a signed artist where the world can hear my music. Below there will be my orignal music I have been working on for the last 6 months, they will just be short demo snippets. With this course I want to help you fast track those 4 years your about to spend with trial and error.

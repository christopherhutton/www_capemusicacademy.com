What To Expect?

This course is deisgned to shortcut your entire learning process in Electronic Music Production. In just 10 lessons, I will share my skills and experience of the last 5 years and fast track you on your journey toward professional music production !  

1

Modules

There are 10 Modules in the first phase of the Electronic Music Production course, the modules are as follows:

1. Toolbar Arrangement, Selection Menu, Channel Rack.
2. Playlist & Story Creation.
3. Mixer Rack.
4. Song Law, Chords & Melody Design, Basic Music Theory.
5. Drums & Sound FX.
6. Sound Design.
7. Automation Clips. 
8. Mixing. 
9. Mastering.
10. Installing Plugins, Installing Packs, Export Settings, Publishing.

The modules and course design are based on the leading industry electronic music software, namely: Fruit Loops. Each module is one session.

Important Things To Know 

Requirements:  
- no previous musical expeience is necessary.
- access to a laptop with either a Windows or Mac operating system is required.

Additional Cost:  
The software necessary for this course is provided free of charge as a trial installer with an unlimted trial period. A fully licensed version of the software is available to purchase online direct from www.image-line.com at any time you may choose to do so. However, for the purpose of this course it is not neccesary.

Session Recordings:  
Each session will be recorded and avaiable for online review to re-live your lesson and perhaps perfect something you just didn't catch in class. These videos are exclusively available to students only. 

Student Feedback:
Feedback is provided to all students after each session via email. This feedback is important for tracking progress and providing personalized guidance. 

Parent Feedback:
After each session, all parents will receive feedback via mail including:
- course content covered in that day's session.
- any exported work your child may present you regarding that days session.
- any personlized feedback (if necessary)
- online access to that day's session recording.
- a means to direct any questions you may have.
